About Us

At Jangid Stone & Interior, we have been redefining luxury in stone craftsmanship since 2002. With a deep-rooted passion for precision and excellence, we specialize in premium stone installation, polishing, inlay work, CNC work, and façade solutions for high-end residential and commercial projects.

Our journey began with a commitment to blending traditional artistry with modern techniques, ensuring that every project is a masterpiece of durability and elegance. Over the years, our expertise and attention to detail have earned us the trust of clients who seek unmatched quality and sophistication in their spaces.

With a dedicated team of skilled artisans and project managers, we take pride in transforming visions into reality, delivering bespoke stone solutions that enhance architectural beauty and add lasting value. At Jangid Stone & Interior, every project is a reflection of our unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and client satisfaction.

Meet our Founder

With over 22 years of experience, Deendayal Jangid has been a driving force in the stone and marble industry, renowned for his expertise in high-end installations and innovative craftsmanship. Since founding Jangid Stone & Interior in 2011, his vision has been to blend traditional artistry with modern techniques, delivering unmatched quality in every project.

His journey began with a deep passion for stone craftsmanship and an understanding that excellence comes from mastering both the material and the craft. Over the years, he has successfully led the company to become a trusted name in this industry, working on some of the most prestigious residential and commercial projects.

Under his leadership, Jangid Stone & Interior has built a legacy of precision, durability, and timeless elegance. His commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction continues to push boundaries, ensuring that every space reflects excellence and sophistication.

Our Mission

At Jangid Stone & Interior, our mission is to transform spaces with superior stone solutions. We are dedicated to delivering exceptional installation and restoration services that enhance the beauty, durability, and value of residential and commercial properties.

Our Approach

We believe in a client-first approach, combining traditional artistry with modern techniques to create timeless designs. Every project is executed with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring a seamless experience from concept to completion.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in the stone industry, recognized for our commitment to precision, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We strive to set new benchmarks in quality, craftsmanship, and service excellence across the country.
